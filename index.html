About

Intricate and stripped down acoustic guitar music. The style is simultaneously raw and refined. Arpeggiated guitar lines flutter and at times may seem to falter and stagger, however, always propulsive and moving forward. At a certain point you may feel a sense of swaying downwards, the next rising forcefully. Intermittent vocals, often in hushed tones, are not overly intrusive or the main focus. The semi-improvised content ensures it's loose, yet still finely woven.
